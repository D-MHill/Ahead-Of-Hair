Kinky 4c

This hair type is tightly coiled with a zig-zag pattern and tends to be prone to dryness. Regular deep conditioning is essential to keep it moisturized and vibrant.

Coily 4b

This hair type features tight, densely packed curls that can be prone to shrinkage and tangling. Regular moisturization is crucial to combat dryness and retain elasticity.

Relaxed/Straightened

This hair type requires careful maintenance to keep it healthy and vibrant. Chemical relaxers or heat styling tools are typically used to achieve this texture, which can leave the hair prone to dryness and damage.

Curly 3C/4A

This hair type typically consists of well-defined curls that are loose and springy. Moisture is essential to combat dryness and maintain curl definition.

Straight

Naturally straight hair tends to be sleek and smooth by nature, but it still requires specific care to maintain its health and shine.

Wavy 2A/2B

This hair type has a loose, relaxed texture that benefits from specific care to enhance its natural waves and maintain overall health. Regular hydration is essential to prevent dryness and frizz.

Chameleon

If you love changing the colour of your hair, taking care of it is key to maintain your color's brilliance. Regular deep conditioning treatments will restore moisture and prevent dryness.

Curly 2C/3A

This hair type hair is characterized by well-defined curls that range from loose to tight. It requires a thoughtful care routine to maintain its shape and bounce. Hydration is key to combat dryness and frizz.
